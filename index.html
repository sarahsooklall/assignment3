Times Ten Riddles

Steps:

  1. Click 'Begin' to start.
  2. Search for any gif (in the search box).
  3. Press enter, and one gif will appear.
  4. Click the gif:
    • Click the gif 10 times, to display a question.
    • Click the gif another 10 times, to show the answer.
    • Questions and answer will be revealed at multiples of 10 (clicks).

  5. Goal: Reach 100 clicks to show the end page.
